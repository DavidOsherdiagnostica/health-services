# Elder Housing Options in Israel (2026)

## Overview of Options

| Option | Independence Level | Typical Monthly Cost | Typical Entry Cost |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| Living at home with care | Varies | Caregiver: 5,000-15,000 NIS | None |
| Retirement home (diur mugan) | High | 8,000-15,000 NIS | Deposit: 400K-1.8M NIS |
| Assisted living | Medium | 10,000-18,000 NIS | Varies |
| Nursing home (beit avot siudi) | Low | 15,000-30,000 NIS | None typically |
| Dementia ward | Low | 18,000-35,000 NIS | None typically |

## Living at Home with Care

### Government support (gimlat siud)
- 5.5 to 30 hours/week of home care based on ADL level (6 levels)
- Free from Bituach Leumi for eligible elderly

### Foreign caregiver (metapel/et zarim)
- Live-in arrangement typical
- Salary (April 2026): minimum 6,443.85 NIS/month gross for 24/6 (257.75 NIS/day, 35.40 NIS/hour). Total employer cost including social benefits, pension and severance reserves: approximately 7,500-9,000 NIS/month
- Room and board provided in kind (not in cash)
- Requires work permit (heter ha'asaka) from the Population and Immigration Authority (PIBA)
- Gimlat siud hours reduced at Levels 3-6 when employing a foreign worker (see Level table)
- Cash benefit option amounts are lower when employing a foreign worker

### Private home care agency
- Hourly caregivers for part-time needs
- 50-80 NIS/hour depending on hours and agency

## Retirement Homes (Diur Mugan)

### What they offer
- Independent apartment within a managed community
- Shared dining, social activities, medical clinic on-site
- Emergency call system
- Maintenance and security
- Option to receive additional care services as needs increase

### Financial model
- Large upfront deposit (400K-1.8M NIS) -- partially or fully refundable
- Monthly management fee (8,000-15,000 NIS)
- Central Israel more expensive than periphery
- Some operate on rental model without deposit

### Who it's for
- Independent elderly who want community and security
- Couples where one may need more care in the future
- Those who want guaranteed care escalation on-site

## Nursing Homes (Beit Avot Siudi)

### Types of wings
- Frail elderly (tashush guf) -- needs partial physical assistance
- Full nursing (siudi maleh) -- needs constant nursing care
- Mentally frail (tashush nefesh) -- cognitive decline, dementia
- Ventilator-dependent -- intensive medical care

### Costs
- Private: 15,000-30,000 NIS/month
- Government-subsidized: through Ministry of Health "code" (tzofan)
- Semi-private: some government subsidy plus family co-payment

### Government subsidized placement
- Apply through local social services (sherut revaha)
- Ministry of Health provides a "code" for qualifying individuals
- Eligibility based on medical need AND financial need
- Waiting lists can be significant in central Israel

### What to look for when choosing
- Government licensing status (check with Ministry of Health)
- Staff-to-resident ratio
- Medical staff qualifications and availability
- Cleanliness and maintenance
- Activity programming
- Location relative to family
- Specific experience with the elder's condition (dementia, physical disability)
