# Israeli Elder Care Benefits Reference (2026)

## Old-Age Pension (Kiztavat Zikna) -- Bituach Leumi

### Retirement Age (2026)
- Men: 67
- Women: 63 in 2026 (for women born 1962). Phases up by birth year: 63y 3m (born 1963), 63y 6m (1964), 63y 9m (1965), 64 (1966), 64y 3m (1967), 64y 6m (1968), 64y 9m (1969), 65 (1970+).

### Monthly Amounts (January 2026)
- Basic individual pension (up to 80): 1,838 NIS
- Individual pension age 80+: 1,941 NIS (includes 103 NIS age supplement)
- Couple base (one earner): 2,762 NIS
- Spouse supplement: 924 NIS
- Child supplement: 581 NIS (first 2 children only)
- Seniority supplement (vatik): 2%/year of insurance over 10 years, max 50%; brings individual to 2,757 NIS
- Health insurance deduction: -237 NIS (individual), -340 NIS (couple)

### Income Supplement Eligibility (2026)
- Individual income threshold: 4,375 NIS/month
- Couple income threshold: 6,912 NIS/month

### Income Test
- Under 70: pension is means-tested (income from work reduces pension)
- 70+: everyone receives pension regardless of income
- Income supplement (hashlamat hachnasa) available for low-income elderly

## Long-Term Care Benefit (Gimlat Siud) -- Bituach Leumi

### Eligibility
- Reached retirement age
- Lives at home (NOT in a nursing institution)
- Needs help with ADL (activities of daily living)
- Passes assessment by Bituach Leumi assessor

### Benefit Levels

| Level | ADL Points | Weekly Hours | With Foreign Worker | Typical ADL Profile |
|-------|-----------|-------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| 1 | 2.5-3 | 5.5 | 5.5 | Needs help with 1-2 ADL areas |
| 2 | 3.5-4.5 | 10 | 10 | Needs help with 3-4 ADL areas |
| 3 | 5-6 | 17 | 14 | Needs help with most ADL areas |
| 4 | 6.5-7.5 | 21 | 18 | Needs help with nearly all ADL areas |
| 5 | 8-9 | 26 | 22 | Dependent in most ADL areas |
| 6 | 9.5-10.5 | 30 | 26 | Fully dependent in all ADL areas |

### Services Included
- Home caregiver visits
- Adult day care center attendance
- Laundry service
- Absorbent products (incontinence supplies)
- Emergency button monitored service
- Supportive community (kehila tomechet)

### Cash Benefit Option (Kitzva b'Kesef)
Recipients can take part or all of the benefit as monthly cash. Max amounts from 01.04.2026:

| Level | Foreign Worker | Israeli Worker |
|-------|----------------|----------------|
| 1 | 1,705 NIS | 1,705 NIS |
| 2 | 2,480 NIS | 2,480 NIS |
| 3 | 3,472 NIS | 4,216 NIS |
| 4 | 4,464 NIS | 5,208 NIS |
| 5 | 5,456 NIS | 6,448 NIS |
| 6 | 6,448 NIS | 7,440 NIS |

Hour-to-cash conversion (April 2026): Level 1 = 310 NIS/hour; Levels 2-6 = 248 NIS/hour.

Full cash requires documented full-time caregiver (12+ hours/day, 6 days/week, non-family, written contract).

### Means Test
- Below threshold: full benefit
- Between threshold and 1.5x: half benefit (Level 1 = 829 NIS; Level 6 = 3,618 NIS cash)
- Above 1.5x: no benefit
- Couples combined as single household

### Exclusions
- Does NOT cover nursing home costs
- Does NOT cover foreign caregiver salary directly (partial offset via cash option only)
- Stops when person moves to a nursing institution

### Application
- Phone: *6050 or local Bituach Leumi branch
- Assessor visits home to evaluate functional level
- Decision typically within 30-60 days

## Private Nursing Care Insurance (Bituach Siudi)

### Key Rules
- Purchased ONLY through kupot cholim (or private companies as standalone policies)
- No statutory age cap, but enrolling by age 49 guarantees max benefits
- Premiums rise sharply with age; above 70 the premium often exceeds expected benefit
- Since December 2023: new joiners only get the basic tier; expanded tier frozen until January 2028
- Free for minors under 18
- Can switch kupot cholim without underwriting or waiting period
- Lower premiums at younger enrollment ages

### What It Provides
- Monthly cash benefit when person becomes ADL-dependent
- Amount varies by plan and kupat cholim
- Can be received simultaneously with gimlat siud
- Typically covers nursing home or home caregiver costs

### The Four Kupot Cholim
- Clalit Mushlam/Platinum
- Maccabi Zahav/Sheli
- Meuhedet Adif/Magen
- Leumit Zahav/Kesef

Each offers different siudi plans with different coverage levels and premiums.
